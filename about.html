Welcome to KidsCare Kenya

KidsCare Kenya is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children and communities in Kenya. Founded on the belief that every child deserves a bright future, KidsCare Kenya focuses on providing essential services and support to underprivileged children, families, and schools across the country.


Through various programs and initiatives, KidsCare Kenya aims to address key areas such as education, healthcare, nutrition, and social development. They work closely with local communities, schools, and government agencies to implement sustainable projects that make a lasting impact.


One of the primary objectives of KidsCare Kenya is to ensure that every child has access to quality education. They provide educational resources, school supplies, and scholarships to disadvantaged students, enabling them to pursue their dreams and build a better future for themselves and their communities.
